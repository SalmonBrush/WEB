Bye Bye World is a creative studio oasis in Kreuzberg where diverse musicians and producers are welcome to create their best work using state-of-the-art recording equipment, in a calm and relaxing environment.

Equipped with a spacious live room, an advanced control room, and a comfortable listening room, Bye Bye World is designed for a wide range of purposes. With instruments to create, rooms to perform together, and tools to give your record the final touch, Bye Bye World is the place where you leave Earth to return with record-ready sounds.
